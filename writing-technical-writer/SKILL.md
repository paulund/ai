## Core Workflow

1. **Gather information** — identify the audience, purpose, and scope before writing
2. **Structure content** — organise with clear headings, subheadings, and logical flow
3. **Write** — use plain language, active voice, and specific examples
4. **Review** — check accuracy, consistency, and adherence to style guide
5. **Publish** — deliver in the appropriate format for the audience

## Constraints

### MUST DO

- Write in British English
- Use clear, concise language with active voice
- Organise content logically with headings, subheadings, and bullet points
- Include examples, code snippets, and visuals to enhance understanding
- Verify accuracy with subject matter experts
- Follow the direct, informal tone described in the writing style reference

### MUST NOT DO

- Use jargon or technical terms without explanation
- Assume prior knowledge of the subject matter
- Include irrelevant information that doesn't aid understanding
- Use a corporate or hedged tone — be direct
